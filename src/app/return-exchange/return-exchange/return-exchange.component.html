Return & Exchange Policy

Shop with Confidence - Easy Returns & Exchanges

1. Introduction

At RaylomShop, your satisfaction is our top priority. If, for any reason, you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we invite you to review our comprehensive return and exchange policy below.

7-Day Return Policy

We offer a hassle-free 7-day return policy on most products. Shop with confidence knowing you can return or exchange items that don't meet your expectations.

2. Order Cancellation Rights

You are entitled to cancel your order within 7 days without providing any reason for doing so. The deadline for cancelling an order is 7 days from the date on which you received the goods.

Timeframe: 7 days from delivery date
No Questions Asked: Cancel for any reason within the return window

3. Conditions for Returns

In order for the goods to be eligible for a return, please ensure that:

  • The goods were purchased within the last 7 days from the date of delivery
  • The goods are in the original packaging with all tags, labels, and accessories intact
  • The goods are unused, unwashed, and in their original condition without any signs of wear or damage
  • All original tags and labels are attached to the product
  • You have the original invoice or proof of purchase

4. Goods That Cannot Be Returned

For health, safety, and hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned or exchanged:

  • Personalized or Custom-Made Items: Goods made to your specifications or clearly personalized
  • Hygiene Products: Goods that are not suitable for return due to health protection or hygiene reasons and were unsealed after delivery (e.g., innerwear, cosmetics, perfumes)
  • Mixed Items: Goods that are, after delivery, inseparably mixed with other items
  • Damaged or Used Items: Items that have been damaged, used, or altered by the customer

5. How to Return or Exchange

Returning or exchanging an item is simple and straightforward. Follow these steps:

1

Initiate Return Request

Log into your account and go to "My Orders". Select the item you want to return and click "Return/Exchange".

2

Select Reason

Choose the reason for return/exchange from the dropdown menu. This helps us improve our service.

3

Package the Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all tags and accessories. Include the original invoice.

4

Schedule Pickup

Our logistics partner will contact you to schedule a convenient pickup time. You can also drop off at our nearest service center.

5

Receive Refund/Exchange

Once we receive and verify the item, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days or dispatch your exchange item.

6. Refund Policy

We process refunds promptly once your return is approved. Here's what you need to know:

  • Processing Time: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and verify the returned item
  • Refund Method: Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the purchase
  • Shipping Charges: Original shipping charges are non-refundable. Return shipping charges may apply
  • Partial Refunds: If only part of your order is returned, you'll receive a partial refund for the returned items only

7. Exchange Policy

Need a different size, color, or style? We offer easy exchanges within the 7-day return window:

  • Same Product: Exchange for a different size or color of the same product
  • Price Difference: If the new item costs more, you'll pay the difference. If it costs less, you'll receive a refund for the difference
  • Availability: Exchanges are subject to product availability. If your desired size/color is out of stock, we'll process a refund
