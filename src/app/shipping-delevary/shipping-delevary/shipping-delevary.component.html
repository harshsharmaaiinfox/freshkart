3. Shipping Methods

We partner with trusted logistics providers to ensure safe and timely delivery:

  • Standard Shipping: Regular delivery service covering all major cities and towns

  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for most locations - pay when you receive your order

7. Delivery Locations

We deliver to locations across India:

  • Metro Cities: Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, and other major cities
  • Tier 2 & 3 Cities: We deliver to most tier 2 and tier 3 cities across India
  • Remote Areas: Delivery to remote areas may take additional time. Please check serviceability at checkout.
Important:

Please ensure your delivery address is complete and accurate. Incorrect addresses may lead to delivery delays or failed deliveries. We are not responsible for delays caused by incorrect address information.

8. Delivery Instructions

To ensure smooth delivery, please keep the following in mind:

  • Available Recipient: Please ensure someone is available at the delivery address during business hours to receive the order
  • Contact Number: Keep your registered mobile number active and accessible for delivery coordination
  • Inspection: Please inspect the package before accepting delivery. Report any damage immediately
  • Failed Delivery: If delivery fails due to unavailability, we'll attempt delivery again. Multiple failed attempts may result in order cancellation
